Hello there, I'm Wade Michalski and I'm about to tell you a smidge about myself. I have always loved creating. When I was a kid I would tape my drawings to the back of my parents' couch and hold mini "art shows". As a teenager, I joined the graphic arts club and my love for creating found direction. By the time I graduated college, I couldn't even go to a restaurant without examining the typeface choices on the menu. Now I'm 28, with over 4 years of experience under my belt and I'm going back to school to learn full stack development. Why development? I'm cheesy for saying this, but I want to design the best digital experiences that any index finger or thumb have ever navigated. In order to do that, I need to be able to speak the language of what I'm designing for - in this case, it's Javascript.

I have a strong background in Design with a BFA in Graphic Design from Minnesota State University - Mankato and 5 years of experience working as a professional designer. I am looking to transition from graphic design to UX/UI, Product Design or Front-End Development.

I am currently taking a Full Stack Development with MERN course at MITxPro where I am learning all the components of web development through the MERN stack. The languages I am proficient in at the moment are HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
